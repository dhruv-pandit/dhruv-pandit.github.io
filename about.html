about me.


Hi, I'm Dhruv. I'm a Master's student in Applied Econometrics in Lisbon, who also enjoys creating content on the side.

I graduated with a BSc in Liberal Arts and Sciences, where I specialised in Mathematics. During my bachelors, I took a keen interest in the Gaelic Games, and played in numerous tournaments with the Maastricht Gaels. In my final year, I worked with the club to design and publish their club website, and served as the Head of PR for the organisation as well. I developed my data anayltical and visualisation skills in my semester long bachelor thesis titled 'Improved order picking and storage strategies in automotive parts warehousing'.

With the tournaments being in different cities, and living in central Europe, I've grown a fondness for traveling and visiting new countries. As a foodie, trying out new dishes is something I'm very passionate about, and hope to make more content on in the future.
